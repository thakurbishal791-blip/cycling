How it works

Three steps to the start line

1. Register interest

Tell us your name and email so we can let you know when booking opens in your city.

2. Book your hour

Choose a one-hour slot at one of ten pop-up locations. Ride solo or enter with your club.

3. Ride & rank

Your bike tracks distance and power output live. Top riders in every age group and gender, plus the best club, go through to the national final.

Event organisers

Manage participants in the admin area

Event staff can log in to search participants and clubs, update power output and distance, and manage entries across all ten locations.
